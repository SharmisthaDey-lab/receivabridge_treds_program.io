Critical-path logic Modules tagged Critical sit on the longest lead-time chain — GSTN access (8–12 wks), MonetaGo membership, NACH sponsor, CERSAI API, CERT-In audit slot. Slip on any of these directly delays RBI authorisation.
RBI mandate coverage Every L2 module maps to TReDS Guidelines 2018 §22(c) — fully electronic platform, real-time MIS, BCP/DR, online surveillance — plus Circular June 2023 features (insurance · secondary market · NACH for non-financed FUs).
Zero-trust posture mTLS between every microservice. AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, MFA for all participants, role-based access, and CERT-In empanelled MSSP overseeing 24×7 SOC operations.
